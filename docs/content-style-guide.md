# Konnekt Content Style Guide

## Competitor Analysis (March 2026)

- **Footer:** `••• \n 🖤 [The Česko](link) — о жизни в Чехии`
- **Style:** Clean, informative, neutral tone
- **Strengths:** Own website, hashtag system (#дайджест, #афиша), daily digests
- **Content:** News, events, food/restaurants, jobs

- **Footer:** `___\n[Pozor!Brno®](link) - новости и жизнь в Чехии 🐊`
- **Style:** More opinionated, engaging, casual
- **Strengths:** High engagement, strong brand (® + croc emoji), Brno focus

### @czech_info (~3.5k views/post)
- **Footer:** `____\n🇨🇿 [**Новости Чехии**](link)`
- **Style:** Detailed, newspaper-like, sometimes uses reaction polls
- **Strengths:** Detailed writing, interactive elements (polls)
- **Content:** News, culture, weather, daily digest

## Our Brand: Konnekt

### Positioning
- **Target:** CIS students in Czechia (CVUT, UK, VSE, VUT, MUNI, VSCHT...)
- **Differentiator:** Student-focused, more informal, useful for daily student life
- **Tone:** Friendly, slightly witty, informative but not boring
- **Language:** Russian (primary), with Czech terms kept where useful

### Post Structure

```
[emoji] [bold headline]

[1-3 paragraphs of content]

[optional: source link or details]

——
🔗 Konnekt | @konnekt_channel
```

### Footer Pattern
```
——
🔗 Konnekt | @konnekt_channel
```

Simple, clean, distinctive. The `——` (em-dash pair) is our separator — different from competitors' `•••`, `___`, `____`.

### Content Categories & Tags
- 📰 News — Czech news relevant to students/expats
- 🎓 Education — University news, deadlines, scholarships
- 💼 Jobs — Student jobs, internships
- 🎉 Events — Student events, cultural events
- 🏠 Life — Housing, transport, health, tips
- 💰 Finance — Student budget tips, discounts, deals
- ⚡ Quick — Short news bites

### Formatting Rules
1. **Always bold headline** — one sentence, punchy
2. **Emoji prefix** — one relevant emoji before headline
3. **Czech terms** — keep official Czech names (Nejvyšší soud, not "Supreme Court") with Russian explanation when needed
4. **Links** — inline, not bare URLs
5. **Length** — 300-500 chars for news, up to 700 for detailed analysis. Hard limit 900 chars (Telegram photo caption)
6. **No exclamation overload** — max 1 per post
7. **Footer** — always present, always last

### Tone Examples

**Good:**
> 🎓 **CVUT продлил дедлайн подачи заявок на стипендию до 15 марта**
>
> Если вы ещё не подали заявку на социальную стипендию, есть хорошая новость — дедлайн сдвинули на две недели. Подробности и форма на сайте университета.
>
> ——
> 🔗 Konnekt | @konnekt_channel

**Bad (too formal):**
> Уважаемые студенты! Администрация ČVUT сообщает о продлении срока подачи заявлений...

**Bad (too casual):**
> ааа братцы дедлайн продлили!!! бегом подавать!!!

### Visual Identity
- No heavy emoji spam (1-2 per post max)
- Clean whitespace between sections
- Consistent footer on every post
